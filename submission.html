Paper Preparation Guidelines

Papers should describe original research. Ongoing or recently completed works are welcome. All submissions will be reviewed by multiple members of a highly qualified scientific committee.

Submission Categories

  • Short papers: Contributions describing original ongoing research works, consisting of up to 6 pages, in English (preferably), or Portuguese.
  • Full papers: Contributions describing original recently finished research works, consisting of up to 12 pages, in English.
  • Databases and datasets: Contributions describing a geospatial dataset organized by its authors, consisting of up to 6 pages.
  • Tools: Contributions describing original tools and applications, consisting of up to 6 pages.

Contributions must be submitted in PDF format, made in accordance with the TO BE ANNOUNCED guidelines. Templates available here.

Submission Instructions

The Microsoft CMT service is used for managing the peer-reviewing process for GEOINFO 2026.

Instructions for using CMT

  1. Access the link to the CMT system.
  2. If you have not used the CMT system before, create an account.
  3. Log into your account.
  4. Select your role as "Author", and the conference "GEOINFO2026".
  5. Click on "+ Create new submission" under the "Author Console".
